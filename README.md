# Sublime Pathways LLC - Website

A modern, bold, and creative website for **Sublime Pathways LLC**, a Residential Treatment Center for young girls.

## About

Sublime Pathways is dedicated to providing comprehensive care for young girls facing challenges of abuse, neglect, and abandonment. The center offers childcare services, treatment services, psychotherapy, medication management, cognitive behavioral therapy, and case management.

**Tagline:** Heal · Grow · Thrive

## Pages

- **Home** - Interactive landing page with hero section, mission pillars, who we serve, services overview, and CTA
- **Services** - Detailed breakdown of all services including childcare, treatment, psychotherapy, medication management, CBT, and case management
- **Admission** - Step-by-step admission process, eligibility information, and inquiry form
- **About Us** - Company story, vision & mission, core values, and therapeutic approach
- **Contact Us** - Contact form, location map, contact information, and FAQ section
